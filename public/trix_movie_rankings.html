The Best Movies According to Trix

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Good Will Hunting

The story is very interesting. A lot of emotional value and lessons from the movie.
Robin Williams performance is very good.

The Internship

Very fun, light-hearted movie. Great to watch, and very motivating.
One of the few movies I will occasionally watch.

Harry Potter: Half-Blood Prince

Pinnacle for the series itself. Very dark and eerie.
All the Harry Potter movies are very good, this is just my favorite.
